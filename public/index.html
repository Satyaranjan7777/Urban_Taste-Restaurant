Welcome to The "Urban Taste"

Where every meal is crafted with care, warmth, and authentic flavors. Step inside and discover a place where good food meets a peaceful, comforting ambience.

At The Urban Taste, we believe in serving dishes made with fresh ingredients, traditional recipes, and a modern touch. Whether you’re here for a family dinner, a cozy date, or a casual bite, we promise a delicious experience you’ll always remember.

Our Speciality
Mix Burger
A juicy, freshly grilled patty tucked inside a soft toasted bun, topped with crisp veggies, melted cheese, and our special house sauce.
Flameburst Biryani
A rich and aromatic biryani cooked on dum with tender meat, fragrant basmati rice, and a burst of smoky, spicy flavors in every bite.
Volcano Tandoori Chicken
Fiery, smoky tandoori chicken with bold spices and a hot, lava-style finish.
Aloo Paratha
Soft, golden parathas stuffed with spiced potato filling, served hot with fresh curd and a dollop of melting butter.
